Zap Dispatch

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Zap Dispatch ("Zap Dispatch," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by Zap Dispatch LLC, based in Florida, USA. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through the Zap Dispatch TMS application (app.zapdispatch.com) and related pages, how we use it, and the choices you have. By creating an account or using Zap Dispatch, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Who this applies to

Zap Dispatch is used by two kinds of accounts: dispatch companies that manage carriers on behalf of a fleet, and carrier companies that manage their own drivers, owner operators, trucks and loads. Each account only sees the data that belongs to its own organization. This policy covers both account types, plus any driver, owner operator, or customer whose information is entered into the system by an account holder.

2. Information we collect

We collect the information you and your organization choose to enter into Zap Dispatch, plus limited technical data needed to run the service:

  • Account information: the email address and password you use to sign in (handled by our authentication provider, Supabase), your company name, and your account type (dispatcher or carrier).
  • Business records you enter: carrier profiles, broker contacts, driver and owner operator records, trucks and trailers, loads and shipment details, rates, dispatch commission percentages, load costs, notes, and documents you upload (such as rate confirmations and proof of delivery).
  • Location and Hours of Service (HOS) data: if your organization connects an Electronic Logging Device (ELD) or GPS provider (such as Apollo ELD or Next Fleet), we receive and store vehicle location, driver duty status, and HOS clock information from that provider so it can be shown on your dashboard and map.
  • Billing information: subscription and payment processing is handled by Stripe. Zap Dispatch does not receive or store your full card number; Stripe shares only what is needed to manage your subscription (such as plan status and payment history).
  • Document contents: if you use the rate confirmation (RateCon) upload feature, the document is sent to Google's Gemini AI service to extract load details automatically. We do not use uploaded documents to train third-party AI models beyond what is required to return the extraction result to you.
  • Usage and device data: basic technical information such as browser type, session activity, and cached app data stored on your device (used to make the app work offline and load faster).

3. How we use this information

  • To operate the core features of the TMS: dispatch, load tracking, invoicing, fleet records, driver links, and reporting.
  • To keep your organization's data separate and secure from other dispatch companies and carriers using Zap Dispatch.
  • To process subscription billing and manage your 30-day free trial and paid plan.
  • To provide customer support and respond to questions you send us.
  • To maintain and improve the security and reliability of the service.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your business records or documents for advertising.

4. Who we share information with

We only share information with the service providers needed to run Zap Dispatch, and only to the extent necessary for them to perform their function:

  • Supabase — database hosting, authentication, and secure storage for your account and business records.
  • Cloudflare — hosting and delivery of the application itself.
  • Stripe — subscription billing and payment processing.
  • Google (Gemini AI) — automatic extraction of data from rate confirmation documents you choose to upload.
  • ELD/GPS providers (such as Apollo ELD or Next Fleet) — if your organization connects one, to retrieve vehicle location and Hours of Service information.
  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles used to display vehicle and load locations.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect the rights and safety of Zap Dispatch or its users, or in connection with a business transfer (such as a merger or acquisition), in which case we will make reasonable efforts to notify affected users.

5. Data isolation and security

Zap Dispatch enforces organization-level data isolation at the database level (row-level security), so one dispatch company or carrier cannot see another's carriers, drivers, loads, or financial records. Access between dispatcher and carrier views is controlled by account permissions. All traffic to the app is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

6. Free access and invitations

Zap Dispatch offers free access only through direct invitation from an authorized account. If you were invited, the information above still applies to your account in the same way as a paying subscriber.

7. Data retention

We retain your account and business records for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward in case you wish to reactivate or as required for legitimate business, legal, or accounting purposes (for example, historical invoices are not altered or deleted after they are issued). You may request deletion of your account and associated data as described in Section 9, subject to any records we are required to keep by law.

8. Children's privacy

Zap Dispatch is a business tool intended for use by adults operating or working with a trucking or dispatch company. It is not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your choices and rights

You can review and update most of your business records directly inside the app. To request a copy of your data, correct inaccurate information, or request deletion of your account, contact us using the information below. We will respond within a reasonable time and may need to verify your identity before making changes.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as Zap Dispatch changes. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continuing to use Zap Dispatch after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact us at rolando@zapdispatch.com.
